DualEnergy builds the software that makes large-scale agrivoltaics bankable, deployable, and operable. We combine regulatory-grade agronomic simulation with field-deployed sensor networks to turn dual-use land projects from speculative bets into financeable assets.

Headquartered in Switzerland. 

Working with developers, asset owners, and research institutions across Europe and North America.
